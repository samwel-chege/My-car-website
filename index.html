Audi etron Gt

Why purchase the Audi

  1. Good perfomance
  2. Exciting looks
  3. Delivers gut-punch acceleration
  4. Composed ride and handling

Why consider

  1. Low backseat headroom
  2. Driving range way off Tesla models
  3. Very light steering effort
  4. Limited rear visibility

Overview

Audi etron Gt is one of the best electric cars in the market right now.In comparison with other electric cars the etron Gt wins
it all in terms of styling, perfomance and pace.Although the etron Gt is arguably the Taycan sister the Audi stands out with its
body work and the high end built-in technology.

Let's have a look at the perfomance figures

  • Top speed: 155mph
  • 0-60mph: 3.0sec
  • 0-100mph: 6.6sec
  • 1/4 mile: 11.1sec

Transmision

  • 1-speed direct-drive
  • 2-speed automatic rear drive
